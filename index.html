朱胜火

Shenghuo ZHU

Shenghuo Zhu is a partner of AiBee, a company on the mission to empower businesses with AI solutions. He was the head of Decision Intelligence Laboratory of DAMO Academy at Alibaba Group. He worked as a senior researcher at NEC Laboratories America and as an engineer on customer behavior research at Amazon.com. He received his Ph.D degree in Computer Science from University of Rochester in 2003.

He is currently working on machine learning and optimization for e-commerce and infrastructure utilization. In addition he is interested in the areas of computer vision, data mining, information retrieval, social computing, user modeling, machine translation, natural language processing, etc.
